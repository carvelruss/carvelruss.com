Job Description

CodeFrites is seeking talented and creative web developers to join our team for an exciting series of website redesign projects.

As a Web Developer at CodeFrites, you will be responsible for revamping and enhancing the user interface and experience of multiple websites using the Bootstrap CSS Framework. Your role will also involve incorporating additional functionalities using Vanilla JavaScript, ensuring a seamless and visually appealing web presence for our clients.

Skills Required

  • Proficient in HTML5 and CSS3
  • Strong command of Vanilla JavaScript
  • Experience with Bootstrap CSS Framework
  • Solid understanding of responsive web design principles
  • Creative and detail-oriented approach to design
  • Excellent problem-solving skills
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills
  • Ability to work independently and meet project deadlines

Tech Stacks

HTML5 CSS3 VanillaJS Bootstrap CSS Framework

Candidate Requirements

  • Proven experience in web development, with a portfolio showcasing previous projects
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Web Development, or a related field (or equivalent experience)
  • Demonstrated ability to work in a collaborative team environment
  • Up-to-date knowledge of industry trends and best practices
  • Ability to adapt to evolving technologies and methodologies
  • Excellent time management and organizational skills

Job Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with the design team to understand project requirements and objectives
  • Implement website redesigns using the Bootstrap CSS Framework
  • Enhance user experience through creative and functional design solutions
  • Develop and integrate new features using Vanilla JavaScript
  • Ensure cross-browser compatibility and responsiveness of websites
  • Debug and troubleshoot issues to optimize site performance
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies
  • Communicate progress and challenges to the project team
  • Collaborate with other developers and stakeholders to achieve project goals

About Code Frites

CodeFrites is a dynamic and innovative web development agency committed to delivering high-quality solutions to our clients. We take pride in our collaborative work environment, where creativity and technical expertise come together to create exceptional digital experiences.

As a member of the CodeFrites team, you will have the opportunity to work on diverse and challenging projects, expand your skill set, and contribute to the success of our clients' online presence. Join us in shaping the future of web development and be a part of a passionate team dedicated to excellence.
